The one-line read

Only 7 in 100 people who reach a page add to cart, and only 1 in 4 who start checkout finish. Top-of-funnel is healthy — story-video ads pull 28–35% CTR at $12–37 CPM. So the fix is page, offer and checkout, not more hooks. Every recommendation below is pointed at that gap.
